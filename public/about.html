This website is to help students at Trinity College navigate the past examination papers more efficiently.

Find modules that have past examination papers you want quicker access to and add them to your-modules by ticking the box beside them. When you visit the home-page in future, the modules you have ticked will be shown there and you can get to past papers in less clicks.

Some courses have past questions split into topics, so that you can quickly access past questions on a specific topic you have been studying.
If you have a genuinely deep understanding of the past papers for a particular module (like say for instance, you wrote them...) and you would like to help splitting the past question into topics (like modules here), then please get in touch.

If you have any useful links for modules, have bugs to report, or have any general feedback, please contact nating@tcd.ie

We hope you find the website helpful!
